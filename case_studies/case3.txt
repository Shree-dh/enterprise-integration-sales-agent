Project Name: Multi-System CRM Integration
Client Industry: Manufacturing
Pain Points: Salesforce disconnected from ERP, 
sales team using manual spreadsheets, no single 
view of customer data across systems
Current Tech Stack: Salesforce CRM, SAP ERP, 
Oracle Database, Excel spreadsheets
Solution: MuleSoft integration platform,
bi-directional sync between Salesforce and SAP,
unified customer dashboard
Recommended Architecture: Salesforce → MuleSoft → SAP ERP → Dashboard
Outcome: Single source of truth for customer data,
sales team productivity increased by 40%
Timeline: Delivered in 12 weeks
