Client Meeting Transcript
Date: March 27, 2026
Participants: John Smith (Client), Sarah (Sales Engineer)

Sarah: Thanks for joining today John. Can you tell me about 
the challenges you're currently facing?

John: Sure. Our biggest problem is that our warehouse 
management system doesn't talk to our ERP system at all. 
Every night, someone manually exports a CSV file and 
uploads it to SAP. It takes about 3 hours and we get 
data errors almost every week.

Sarah: What systems are you currently using?

John: We use SAP for our ERP, Oracle for our warehouse 
management, and we recently started using Salesforce 
for our sales team. Everything is completely disconnected.

Sarah: What would be your ideal outcome if we solved this?

John: We want real-time data sync between all three systems. 
No more manual work, no more errors. Ideally everything 
talks to each other automatically. We also want a 
dashboard to monitor the data flow.

Sarah: How many transactions are we talking about daily?

John: Around 5,000 orders per day. During peak season 
it goes up to 15,000. The manual process just cannot 
handle that volume anymore.

Sarah: Any budget or timeline constraints?

John: We need this done within 3 months. Budget is 
flexible if the solution is solid.
